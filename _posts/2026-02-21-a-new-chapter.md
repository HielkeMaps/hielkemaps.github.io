Parkour Spiral is now on Roblox! 

For the past 2 years, I've been secretly building a Roblox game, and I'm SO excited to finally share it with you!

<!--more-->

After creating Minecraft content for over 10 years, I felt ready to take on a whole new challenge, and honestly? This has been one of the most fun projects I've ever worked on. Roblox gives me SO much more freedom to design full game systems and mechanics, and I've been absolutely loving every second of it.

Think ranked races, leaderboards, ghost runs, and crazy special races like rising lava and moon gravity. It's everything I've always wanted to build but couldn't in Minecraft!

I'd love for you to be part of shaping this project, so if you're curious, check out [hielkestudios.com](https://hielkestudios.com) for more info.
